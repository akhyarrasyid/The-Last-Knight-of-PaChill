# The Last Knight of PaChill
One brave knight, a trusty shotgun, and a horde of explosive slimes. Stranded on the once-peaceful lands of PaChill, you must wield both ancient steel and modern gunpowder to carve a path through slimes and fire-wielding goblins.

<br>

### Game Concept
**The Last Knight of PaChill** is an intense top-down action-survival game where you embody the final line of defense against a relentless tide of mystical creatures. 

As the last knight, your survival depends on mastering the synergy between your swift **Sword** and your heavy-hitting **Shotgun**. The game features a unique "Ticking Proximity" mechanic where enemies like the Slime Bombs become increasingly dangerous as they get closer, signaled by dynamic audio pitch-shifting. Can you clear all 3 levels and set the best time record?

---

### Controls
- **WASD / Arrow Keys**: Move
- **Space**: Dash (Dodge attacks & traverse faster)
- **Left Click**: Sword Slash (Quick close-range attack)
- **Right Click**: Shotgun Blast (Powerful knockback & AOE damage)
- **Esc**: Pause / Menu
